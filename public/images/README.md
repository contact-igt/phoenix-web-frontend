# Phoenix Fitness Image Assets Directory

Please upload/place your fitness service page images here. Below are the filenames expected by the page structure:

1. **Hero Background**: `hero-bg.jpg` (Atmospheric shot of athlete or gym setting)
2. **Strength Section**: `strength.jpg` (Athlete doing barbell lifting, squating, etc.)
3. **Cardio Section**:
   - `cardio-main.jpg` (Main cardio exercise picture)
   - `cardio-sub1.jpg` (Small cardio inset 1)
   - `cardio-sub2.jpg` (Small cardio inset 2)
4. **Functional Section**:
   - `functional-main.jpg` (Main athletic movement picture)
   - `functional-sub1.jpg` (Sub inset 1)
   - `functional-sub2.jpg` (Sub inset 2)
   - `functional-sub3.jpg` (Wide banner inset 3)
5. **Personal Section**: `personal.jpg` (Personal coaching session or training picture)

*Note: All images will automatically use a sleek dark glassmorphism placeholder style with hover effects until they are uploaded!*
